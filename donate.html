Why it matters

The paywall is a political choice.

Most serious historical scholarship is locked behind academic paywalls or institutional subscriptions that cost hundreds of dollars a year. The people who most need access to this history — students in underfunded schools, community organizers, families trying to understand their own story — are the ones least able to pay for it.

Chain exists because we believe that understanding the chain — from African civilizations to present-day inequality — should not require a credit card. Your donation makes that possible for everyone.
