Build a database-driven content management system for Retrieve Your Roots.

Project Name:
Retrieve Your Roots Content Database

Purpose:
Create a structured database that stores historical content from retrieve-your-roots.com and prepares it for automated newsletter generation, Twitter/X posts, social posts, and future AI content workflows.

Core Goal:
This database should become the central “content brain” for the brand. Every historical topic, article, quote, timeline fact, image prompt, newsletter idea, and social post should be stored, searchable, reusable, and ready for automation.
